We are an XR design collective of artists, architects, and technologists creating immersive experiences at the intersection of research, space, and emerging technology. Our work spans exhibitions, digital environments, public installations, and experimental interfaces.

OUR TEAM

Shruti Sharma

Shruti Sharma is an XR designer and researcher. She holds a degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Princeton University, where she focused on immersive media and spatial computing. Her work explores the intersection of technology, storytelling, and human experience, spanning interactive installations, virtual environments, and experimental interfaces that reimagine how people engage with space and narrative.

Yuchen Shi

Yuchen Shi is a multidisciplinary artist based in Princeton. Her practice spans digital media, sculpture, and immersive environments, investigating themes of memory, perception, and the body in technological space. She has exhibited internationally and collaborates across disciplines to create experiential works that blur the boundaries between physical and virtual.

Robin Resch

Robin Resch is a visual artist and photographer whose work examines landscape, identity, and the constructed image. Through photography, installation, and collaborative projects, she explores how visual culture shapes our understanding of place and belonging. She has worked with cultural institutions and independent spaces across Europe and the United States.

Devin Greenwood

Devin Greenwood is an American audio-visual artist and composer working at the intersection of sound, image, and immersive media. His practice includes composition for film and installation, live audiovisual performance, and experimental sound design. He has collaborated with artists, museums, and design studios to create multisensory experiences that heighten spatial and emotional awareness.
